Welcome to your all-in-one health companion! Under our Tracker section, explore powerful tools designed just for you — Sleep Analysis, Diet Tracker, and Workout Plans. Sleep Analysis

Our Sleep Analysis tool gives you a deep understanding of your sleep quality every night. By tracking the total duration of your sleep and breaking it into different stages like light sleep, deep sleep, and REM, it helps you identify how well your body and mind are resting. You’ll get detailed graphs and insights showing when you were in each stage, how often you woke up, and how consistent your sleep patterns are. This data allows you to improve your bedtime routine, reduce stress, and boost your energy levels during the day. Whether you're struggling with falling asleep, waking up frequently, or just want to feel more rested, our Sleep Analysis tool gives you the clarity to take action. With better sleep, your mood, focus, and overall health get better too. It's not just about hours slept — it's about sleeping smarter, and we’re here to guide you every step.

Diet Tracker

Our Diet Tracker is your daily companion for smart and simple nutrition tracking. You can add dishes you eat throughout the day, and for each dish, we provide detailed nutritional values — including calories, protein, fats, and carbohydrates. As you build your daily meals, the tracker instantly calculates your total intake, helping you stay aligned with your dietary goals. Whether you're aiming to lose weight, gain muscle, or just live healthier, our system keeps you informed about exactly what you're putting into your body. You can spot what’s missing or what you’re overdoing, and adjust your meals accordingly. No need for guesswork — the tracker does the math for you. With visual breakdowns and progress tracking, you’ll see how your eating habits evolve over time. Healthy living starts with awareness, and our Diet Tracker gives you the tools to take control of your nutrition and build a balanced lifestyle that works for you.

Workout Tracker

Our Workout Tracker helps you stay consistent, accountable, and motivated on your fitness journey. Log your workouts day by day, track sets, reps, weights, and time spent exercising — all in one place. Whether you're doing strength training, cardio, yoga, or home workouts, the tracker keeps a full history of your progress. You can see which muscle groups you’ve worked, how often you train, and how your performance improves over time. No more guessing what to do next — you’ll know exactly where you left off and how far you’ve come. The tracker also gives visual stats and weekly summaries to keep you pumped and on track with your goals. Whether you’re chasing gains, endurance, or general fitness, our Workout Tracker is your reliable fitness buddy, keeping you focused and consistent. It’s not just about working out — it’s about making every session count, and we’re here to track it with you.
